★ What I'm building: BrokerPlus

BrokerPlus is a vertical SaaS platform for residential mortgage brokers. It scans a broker's client database and surfaces the revenue hiding inside it: refinances, renewals, HELOCs, reverse mortgages, and debt consolidation opportunities, with lender-specific IRD penalty math already calculated. Brokers import from Filogix, Velocity, Finmo, or CSV in minutes, then let the platform handle automated client outreach, branded equity reports, and ongoing FINTRAC/AML screening.
